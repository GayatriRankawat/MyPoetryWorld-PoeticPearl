Shayari/Gazal

Alfazo ka dariya aur ye zindgi


Phir na aaya loutkar brso,
Wo lazim jo aankhon me noor tha...

Nature

Kuch keh rha aasma suno


Aasman ki chadar me kahi
Chand sa ek chehra ho jese

Devoted to God

Ishwar satya hai, satya hi shiv hai


Prarmbh tumse tum jan jan prabhari
Ganpati tum isha santan pyari

Self Love

Muskurao, har dfa muskurao


Manmouzi me khilkhilati hu
Khushiya karib main har dfa muskurati hu

Poetry Magic

Take a Dive into Emotions


I walk alone, she meets
I scare to go, she moves along

Others

Mai zindgi ka sath nibhata chla gya
Har zkhm ko dhue me udata chla gya
